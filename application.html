Play Archer Hero

Overview

Archer Hero is an exciting bow-and-arrow game where players must eliminate enemies using precise shots. The game requires accuracy and strategy to defeat waves of opponents and progress through levels.

How To Play

Follow the steps below on how to play

  1. Use your mouse or touch controls to aim your bow.
  2. Hold and release to adjust the strength of your shot.
  3. Defeat enemies before they reach you.
  4. Progress through levels by eliminating all opponents.

Reference Guide

Aim Mouse movement
Movement WASD or arrow keys
Shoot Space
